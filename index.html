About Me

I am 23 years old and currently a second-year student studying Digital Design with electives in Fine Arts.

My passion for art ignited when I was four years old having the opportunity to get creative often in school art studios. My style was founded in the last year of high-school when I was creating my ATAR piece. After high-school I further developed my skills by testing and trialling different textures, mediums and styles in between University commitments. I fell in love with many methods and styles; I’ve worked with Lino Prints, plasticine paintings, watercolour, acrylic, clay sculpturing, spray painting, Ink etchings, paint pens and some textiles.

I came to realise that texture and colour really intrigued me and so I began experimenting with those. And thus, the early stages of my style was born. This style of mine is fluid and vibrant, full of playful colours that create a visually intriguing experience. I am currently working of furthering this style and truly making it my own. I love working with others so am always keen to collaborate and create some magic!

Artworks

I am always working on 2-3 paintings at a time and really enjoy creating hidden meaning abstract art. My goal for these artworks is to bring truth to society and happiness to those that have the pleasure of taking a piece home with them. I work in a clean and happy environment which I feel is necessary to create an artwork of the like. Art is my passion and I will be creating until my days are done.

The meaning of my art is an illusion, only the viewer can create meaning from it. Every piece carries a high vibration and certain emotional qualities which I want to share with the world.


“The meaning of life is to find your gift, the purpose of life is to give it away”
- Pablo Picasso


My purpose is to spread healing through my art to people all over the world. I feel that my art chooses the buyer; in which I mean my art seeks out people who need it most.
